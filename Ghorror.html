Resident Evil Game and Movies

Resident Evil - The Games

  • Resident Evil 7: Biohazard (2017): A survival horror that returns to the series' roots with first-person perspective and a terrifying family.

  • Resident Evil Village (2021): The eighth major installment in the series, featuring a haunted village and supernatural threats.

Resident Evil - The Movies

  • Resident Evil (2002): The first film in the series, following Alice as she fights her way through the Raccoon City outbreak.

  • Resident Evil: Apocalypse (2004): The second film in the series, following Alice as she fights against both zombies and the Umbrella Corporation.

  • Resident Evil: Extinction (2007): The third film where Alice teams up with survivors in a world ravaged by the T-virus.

  • Resident Evil: Afterlife (2010): The fourth film, where Alice searches for a safe haven from the undead outbreak.

  • Resident Evil: Retribution (2012): The fifth film, where Alice fights to survive in a facility filled with deadly traps.

  • Resident Evil: The Final Chapter (2016): The final movie in the series where Alice makes her last stand against the Umbrella Corporation.

  • Resident Evil: Welcome to Raccoon City (2021): A reboot of the series, following the events of the first two video games.

Silent Hill Game and Movies

Silent Hill - The Games

  • Silent Hill 2 (2001): A psychological horror game that follows James Sunderland as he searches for his deceased wife in a disturbing town.

  • Silent Hill 3 (2003): A continuation from Silent Hill 2, where Heather Mason is thrust into the mysteries of Silent Hill.

  • Silent Hill 4: The Room (2004): The game follows Henry Townshend as he is trapped in his apartment and experiences eerie supernatural occurrences.

Silent Hill - The Movies

  • Silent Hill (2006): A film adaptation of the game series, following a mother searching for her adopted daughter in the terrifying town of Silent Hill.

  • Silent Hill: Revelation (2012): The sequel to Silent Hill, where Heather Mason returns to the town to discover the truth about her father's disappearance.
